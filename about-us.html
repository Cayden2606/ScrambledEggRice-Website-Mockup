About Dan Lao

Welcome to Danlao, home of the authentic Hong Kong Style Scrambled Egg Rice Bowls!


OUR STORY

The name "Danlao" (蛋佬) literally translates to "guy who does eggs" in Mandarin, a fitting description of our speciality. At Danlao, we take pride in crafting delectable rice bowls inspired by the vibrant flavours of Hong Kong, focusing on the humble yet versatile egg as the star ingredient.

Our menu features Hong Kong-inspired scrambled egg rice bowls topped with comforting ingredients like savoury luncheon meat cubes and juicy tomatoes. Each dish is a celebration of simplicity and comfort, delivering flavours that resonate with the soul.

What sets us apart is our commitment to quality. We prepare most of our ingredients in-house, ensuring freshness and authenticity in every bite. Every order is meticulously cooked to perfection, guaranteeing a delightful experience for our valued customers.

Join the hype and experience the sensation that has captivated the taste buds of many. Come savour our scrumptious offerings and discover why Danlao has become a local favourite. We can't wait to share our love for Hong Kong-style comfort food with you!
